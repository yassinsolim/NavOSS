The operating principles

Useful without surveillance.

The first milestone is a focused Calgary navigation experience with transparent providers and conservative safety behavior.

01

Account-free

Search, route preview, and active guidance do not require a profile, social graph, or advertising identifier.

02

Calgary-first

One city earns reliability before coverage expands. Route and search gaps become repeatable tests.

03

Inspectable

Open contracts, visible attribution, explicit degraded states, and measured release gates replace vague parity claims.
